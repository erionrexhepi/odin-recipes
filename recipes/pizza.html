Description

A prosciutto pizza is a savory Italian-inspired dish featuring a crisp crust topped with salty prosciutto

resh mozzarella, and often arugula or other greens, finished with a drizzle of balsamic glaze or olive oil

It combines rich, umami flavors with fresh, bright elements for a balanced and satisfying meal.

Prep Time

  • Prep Time: 15–20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 12–20 minutes
  • Servings: 2–8
  • Temperature: 400°F–550°F (200°C–290°C),

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough: 1 batch (approximately 437g for two 12–13 inch pizzas)
  • Pizza Sauce: ½–⅔ cup (120–160ml) red sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • Fresh Mozzarella: 8 ounces (225g), torn into blobs
  • Prosciutto: 8 slices (approximately 3–4 oz or 85–115g), thinly sliced
  • Arugula: 1½ ounces (about 40g or 2 lightly packed cups)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil: 2–3 tablespoons (30–45ml), plus more for drizzling
  • Lemon Juice: 1 tablespoon (15ml)
  • White Wine Vinegar: 1 tablespoon (15ml)
  • Garlic: 1 clove, finely minced
  • Dijon Mustard: 1 teaspoon (5g)
  • Fresh Basil: 4 large leaves, thinly sliced
  • Shaved Parmesan: 2 tablespoons (15g)
  • Kosher Salt and Freshly Ground Black Pepper: To taste

Equipment Needed:

  • Pizza stone or steel (recommended for best crust)
  • Pizza peel (for transferring pizza)
  • Baking sheet (if no stone/steel)
  • Parchment paper (optional, for easier transfer)
  • Small bowl or jar (for vinaigrette)
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Rolling pin or floured surface (for stretching dough)

Step by Step

  1. Warm water + sugar + yeast – Mix and let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Flour, salt, olive oil – Add to yeast mixture; knead into smooth dough.
  3. Oiled bowl – Place dough in bowl, turn to coat, cover, let rise 1 hour.
  4. Tomato sauce base – Mix passata/tomato paste, garlic, olive oil, oregano, salt.
  5. Dough – Punch down, divide, roll into thin 12-inch rounds on floured surface.
  6. Baking sheet or preheated pizza stone – Transfer dough to surface.
  7. Sauce – Spread evenly, leaving ½-inch border.
  8. Cheese (mozzarella, Parmesan) – Sprinkle over sauce.
  9. Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, etc.) – Add evenly.
  10. Olive oil – Drizzle on crust.

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) with pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Prepare dough and let rise.
  3. Make sauce while dough rises.
  4. Roll out dough and transfer to floured baking sheet or peel.
  5. Top pizza with sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  6. Bake 8–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  7. Rest 2–3 minutes, then slice and serve.
