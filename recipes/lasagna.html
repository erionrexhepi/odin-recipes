Lasagna recipe

Home

Description

Classic lasagna combines rich meat sauce, creamy cheese filling

and tender noodles in a baked casserole that’s perfect for feeding a crowd or meal prepping

The key to success lies in layering flavors, using quality ingredients like whole-milk ricotta and a robust tomato sauce

and letting the lasagna rest after baking for clean slices.

Prep Time

  • Prep time: 35 minutes
  • Cook Time: 55 minutes
  • Servings: 4–6
  • Total time: 1 hour 30 minutes (plus optional chilling time)

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled until al dente)
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • ½ lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 36 oz pasta sauce (or marinara)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp sugar (to balance acidity)
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella, divided
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan, divided
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step by Step

  1. Olive oil – Heat in a Dutch oven or large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Onion, garlic – Sauté until soft
  3. Ground beef, Italian sausage – Add and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
  4. Tomato paste – Stir in and cook 1 minute.
  5. Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, sugar, salt, pepper, basil, oregano, parsley – Add and simmer 30–45 minutes.
  6. Lasagna noodles – Boil in salted water with olive oil until al dente; drain and rinse.
  7. Ricotta, egg, Parmesan, mozzarella, salt, pepper – Mix in a bowl for cheese layer.
  8. Meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, mozzarella, Parmesan – Layer in baking dish.

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Make sauce: Sauté aromatics, brown meat, add tomato paste and canned tomatoes, simmer 30+ minutes.
  3. Cook noodles (unless using oven-ready).
  4. Mix cheese layer with ricotta, egg, Parmesan, mozzarella, and seasonings.
  5. Assemble: Spread sauce on bottom of dish. Layer: noodles → cheese mixture → sauce → mozzarella/Parmesan. Repeat 3–4 times, ending with sauce and top cheese layer.
  6. Bake covered 20 minutes, then uncover and bake 20–25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Rest 15 minutes before serving.
