Description

Baked chicken schnitzel is a healthier,

oven-cooked version of the classic fried dish,

featuring tender chicken breasts coated in a crispy breadcrumb crust.

It's a popular main course with German roots,

often served with sides like potatoes and coleslaw.

Prep Time

  • Prep Time: 15–20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 12–45 minutes (depending on recipe)
  • Servings: 4–6
  • Temperature: 200°C (400°F) to 425°F (220°C)

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts: 2 large (halved horizontally and pounded to even thickness)
  • Panko breadcrumbs: 2 cups (120 g)
  • Plain (all-purpose) flour: ½ cup (75 g)
  • Eggs: 2, whisked
  • Olive oil: 2–3 tbsp (for coating and baking)
  • Sea salt flakes: 1–2 tsp (divided)
  • Freshly cracked black pepper: ½ tsp (divided)
  • Sweet paprika: 1 tsp
  • Lemon zest: Zest of one large lemon (optional, for flavor)
  • Dried sage: 2 tsp (optional, for herbed variation)
  • Potatoes (for wedges): 8 large, cut into wedges (peeled or skin left on)
  • Cabbage (green and red): 1 cup each, shredded
  • Carrot: ½ cup (80 g), grated or julienned
  • Kale: ½ cup (35 g), finely shredded
  • Corn kernels: ½ cup (75 g), frozen or canned
  • Spring onion (scallion): 1, finely chopped
  • Apple cider vinegar: 1 tbsp
  • Lime wedges and coriander (cilantro) sprigs: For serving

Step by Step

  1. Chicken breasts (6, halved) – Butterfly and pound to even thickness. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Flour (¾ cup) + paprika (1 tbsp) – Mix on a plate for dredging.
  3. Eggs (2, beaten) – Place in a shallow bowl for dipping.
  4. Breadcrumbs (2 cups) + lemon zest (1 lemon) – Mix on a separate plate for coating.
  5. Olive oil – Spray or drizzle on baking sheet and over chicken for crispiness.

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with foil and lightly oil it.
  2. Set up station: Flour mix → egg → breadcrumb mix.
  3. Dredge chicken: Coat each piece in flour, dip in egg, then press into breadcrumbs.
  4. Arrange on baking sheet in a single layer.
  5. Spray tops with olive oil.
  6. Bake 5–6 minutes, flip, then bake another 5–6 minutes until golden and internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Serve with lemon wedges.
